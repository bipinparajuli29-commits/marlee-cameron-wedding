How It Began · The First Chapter

A Chance Encounter

Some meetings feel less like coincidence and more like inevitability — as if the universe had quietly been arranging the pieces for years. The first time their eyes met, neither of them knew what was beginning. But something shifted. Something that doesn't have a word yet, only a feeling you'd recognise if it happened to you.

The Journey · Side by Side

Every Road Led Here

Love, they discovered, is not one grand gesture — it is ten thousand quiet ones. It is laughing too loud in strange cities, choosing each other on the unremarkable Tuesdays, and knowing without question that whatever comes next, you want them beside you for all of it.

The Proposal · Annapurna Base Camp, 4,130m

At the Top of the World

He had carried the ring for days through mountain paths and thin air, waiting for the exact right moment. It came at 4,130 metres, with the glaciers of the Annapurna massif blazing white behind them and the whole sky watching. She said yes before he finished asking. The mountains have never looked more beautiful.
